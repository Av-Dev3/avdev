About Me

Hi, I’m Alex. For the last nine years, I’ve worked in the service industry, learning how to stay calm under pressure, solve problems fast, and deal with just about every kind of situation you can imagine. But behind the scenes, I’ve always been drawn to tech. I’d stay up late tinkering with websites, teaching myself code, and dreaming about doing this for real one day.

That day finally came. I decided to stop treating this like a hobby and start treating it like my future.

Right now, I’m focused on becoming a full stack web developer. I’ve been building projects from the ground up with HTML, CSS, and JavaScript, and I recently started working with databases and backend tools like Supabase. I’m creating real tools I actually use, like a productivity planner to help me stay on track.

This site is part of my learning journey. I’m not taking on any work just yet since I’m still in the process of leveling up, but I wanted to put this out there anyway. I believe in building in public and keeping things honest.

Outside of coding, I’m probably bowling, working a shift, or just trying to improve a little every day. Thanks for checking out my site. There’s more to come.
